# Botium Toys Internal Security Audit

## Scope and Goals
The audit covers the full security program including assets, systems, and compliance requirements.

## Key Findings
- Weak access controls (no least privilege or separation of duties)
- No encryption for customer/payment data
- No IDS/IPS system
- No backups or disaster recovery plan
- Weak password management

## Controls Assessment Summary
- Several technical, administrative, and compliance controls are missing
- Existing controls include firewall, antivirus, CCTV, and physical security

## Compliance Issues
- PCI DSS gaps due to lack of encryption and access control
- GDPR risks due to weak data protection and classification

## Recommendations
- Implement least privilege and separation of duties
- Deploy encryption for sensitive data
- Add IDS/IPS and centralized password management
- Establish backups and disaster recovery plan
- Improve password policy enforcement
- Perform full asset inventory and classification
